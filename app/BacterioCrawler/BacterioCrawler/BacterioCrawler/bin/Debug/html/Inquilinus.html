Genus Inquilinus

Warning: In the List of Prokaryotic names with Standing in Nomenclature, an arrow (→) only indicates the sequence of valid publication of names and does not mean that the last name in the sequence must be used (see: Introduction).

Number of species, including synonyms, cited in this file: 2
Number of subspecies, including synonyms, cited in this file: 0

Classification (Warning: see also the file "Classification of prokaryotes: Introduction").

 

Inquilinus Coenye et al. 2002, gen. nov.
Type species: ¤ Inquilinus limosus Coenye et al. 2002.
Etymology: L. masc. n. inquilinus, an inhabitant of a place that is not its own, a sojourner.
Valid publication: Validation List no. 87. Int. J. Syst. Evol. Microbiol., 2002, 52, 1437-1438.
Effective publication: COENYE (T.), GORIS (J.), SPILKER (T.), VANDAMME (P.) and LIPUMA (J.J.): Characterization of unusual bacteria isolated from respiratory secretions of cystic fibrosis patients and description of Inquilinus limosus gen. nov., sp. nov. J. Clin. Microbiol., 2002, 40, 2062-2069.
Validation List no. 87 in IJSEM Online - Effective publication Online

  

Inquilinus ginsengisoli Jung et al. 2011, sp. nov.
Type strain: (see also Global Catalogue of Microorganisms) Gsoil 080 = KCTC 12574 = LMG 23638.
Sequence accession no. (16S rRNA gene) for the type strain: AB245352.
Etymology: N.L. n. ginsengum, ginseng; L. n. solum, soil; N.L. gen. n. ginsengisoli, of soil of a ginseng field, the source of the type strain.
Valid publication: JUNG (H.M.), LEE (J.S.), BAE (H.M.), YI (T.H.), KIM (S.Y.), LEE (S.T.) and IM (W.T.): Inquilinus ginsengisoli sp. nov., isolated from soil of a ginseng field. Int. J. Syst. Evol. Microbiol., 2011, 61, 201-204.
Original article in IJSEM Online

  

Inquilinus limosus Coenye et al. 2002, sp. nov. (Type species of the genus.)
Type strain: (see also Global Catalogue of Microorganisms) AU0476 = LMG 20952 = CCUG 45653.
Sequence accession no. (16S rRNA gene) for the type strain: AY043374.
Etymology: L. masc. adj. limosus, full of slime, slimy.
Valid publication: Validation List no. 87. Int. J. Syst. Evol. Microbiol., 2002, 52, 1437-1438.
Effective publication: COENYE (T.), GORIS (J.), SPILKER (T.), VANDAMME (P.) and LIPUMA (J.J.): Characterization of unusual bacteria isolated from respiratory secretions of cystic fibrosis patients and description of Inquilinus limosus gen. nov., sp. nov. J. Clin. Microbiol., 2002, 40, 2062-2069.
Validation List no. 87 in IJSEM Online - Effective publication Online
